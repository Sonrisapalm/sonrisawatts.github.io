Summary Interpretation

The models suggest a **cognitive-affective consistency** in algorithmic trust. Individuals comfortable with AI in objective roles are more likely to demand explanation (OR=2.96, p=.102). Critically, **Perceived Transparency** significantly increases trust (OR=3.80, p=.038).
