About Emotion Encoded

Hi! I’m Sonrisa Watts, 24, from the beautiful island of St. Kitts and Nevis. I founded Emotion Encoded to investigate AI integration and adoption through the lenses of cognitive bias, psychological resistance, and emotional reasoning.

Our mission is to explore how human perception, culture, and emotion influence the way people trust and adopt AI technologies. We conduct interviews, publish insights, and aim to help align AI systems with human understanding, making AI more transparent, ethical, and human-centered.
