About Sonrisa Watts

I'm Sonrisa Watts, a 24-year-old researcher from the island of St. Kitts and Nevis. I founded Emotion Encoded to explore how emotional reasoning, psychological resistance, and cognitive biases influence AI adoption and integration. My work bridges psychology and technology to foster trust and transparency in AI systems.

With a background in psychology and a passion for technology, I aim to decode the emotional barriers that affect human interactions with AI, ensuring these systems align with human values and societal needs.
