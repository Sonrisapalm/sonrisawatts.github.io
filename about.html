About Emotion Encoded

Emotion Encoded is a research and dialogue platform exploring how emotional reasoning, cultural context, and psychological bias shape human trust in AI systems. Our mission is to bridge the gap between emerging technology and human understanding, ensuring that AI tools are transparent, ethical, and inclusive.

Founded at the intersection of psychology and technology, Emotion Encoded collaborates with professionals in high-stakes industries such as law, healthcare, and finance to investigate how trust is built—or broken—in AI adoption. Through interviews, public engagement, and experimental research, we aim to create actionable frameworks for AI governance and trust-building.

This initiative is led by researcher Sonrisa Watts with a background in psychology, who is now breaking into AI ethics, trust, and human-computer interaction. Our work is part of a broader vision to equip governments, professionals in high-stake fields, organizations, and communities with the insights needed to navigate an AI-driven future responsibly.
