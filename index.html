About

Encoded Emotion investigates how emotional reasoning, cultural context, and psychological bias shape human responses to AI. We aim to foster dialogue and research into AI trust and adoption.

Research

Our projects span cognitive bias simulations, trust metrics for AI, and studies on emotional reasoning in professional AI adoption. We collaborate with researchers, developers, and policymakers.
