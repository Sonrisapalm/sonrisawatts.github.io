Welcome

Emotion Encoded is a research initiative dedicated to understanding how emotional reasoning, cultural context, and psychological bias shape human responses to AI.

Through research and collaboration with professionals in law, medicine, and policy, we aim to foster trust, transparency, and ethical AI governance.
