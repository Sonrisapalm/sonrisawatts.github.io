Emotion Encoded

By Sonrisa Watts: Exploring the intersection of psychology and AI to build trust and transparency.

Welcome

Emotion Encoded is a research initiative dedicated to understanding how emotional reasoning, trust, cognitive biases, cultural context, and other psychological factors shape human responses to AI. This is important in order to facilitate the integration and adoption of AI into industries.

Through research and collaboration with professionals in law, medicine, and policy, we aim to foster trust, transparency, and ethical AI governance.

AI Advisors vs AI Therapists: Who Do We Trust?

I conducted research in the Caribbean Region and I got some interesting answers. Most responses are from my hometown St. Kitts & Nevis. I asked: "Who would you trust more? An AI advisor or an AI Therapist?" Here are some highlights:

  • "Financial advisor is a little too private for me"
  • "A therapist needs a certain level of emotional intelligence that I don't think AI has developed"
  • "Finance is numbers. I can sort out my own feelings"
  • "Therapist work may involve actual human feelings and rational thinking..."
