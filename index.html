About the Project

Encoded Emotion investigates how emotional reasoning, culture, and bias shape our trust in artificial intelligence. We collect stories, run experiments, and design conversations between humans and machines.

Join the Conversation

We work with researchers, policymakers, and everyday people to build AI systems that are transparent, ethical, and trusted. Your voice matters — let’s make AI that understands us.
