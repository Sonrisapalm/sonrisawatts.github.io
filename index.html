Shaping AI people can trust by first understanding the psychological factors that shape human perception of it. By conducting interviews with experts in law, medicine/healthcare, finance, and education, and gaining perceptions and insight from the general population, Emotion Encoded helps to align AI systems with the human mind, not just with data, ensuring their effective adoption.

Emotion Encoded investigates how trust, cognitive bias, heuristics, and judgment distortions shape AI adoption in high-stakes domains. We are dedicated to using Psychology to Decode Emotional Barriers to AI Adoption.
