Emotion Encoded

By Sonrisa Watts: Exploring the intersection of psychology and AI to build trust and transparency.

Welcome

Emotion Encoded is a research initiative dedicated to understanding how emotional reasoning, trust, cognitive biases, cultural context, and other psychological factors shape human responses to AI. This is important in order to facilitate the integration and adoption of AI into industries.

We actively shape transparent governance for AI, working alongside professionals in law, medicine, finance, education, and other important domains to cultivate public trust and ensure systems are just and accountable.

Articles

I've published research on the psychological reasons for resistance to AI, expanding scholarship on the integration of AI in important industries. I also pitched to the Ministry of Legal to initiate the development of AI governance frameworks.
