Welcome

Emotion Encoded is a research initiative based in St.Kitts and Nevis, dedicated to understanding how emotional reasoning, cultural context, and psychological bias shape human responses to AI.

We're shaping AI people can trust by first understanding the psychological factors that shape human perception of it. By collaborating with experts in law, medicine, finance, and education, we help align AI systems with the human mind, not just with data, ensuring their effective adoption
